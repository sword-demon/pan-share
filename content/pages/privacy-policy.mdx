## Introduction

Welcome to **panShare**, a cloud drive resource sharing platform. panShare is dedicated to helping users discover and share quality cloud storage resource links. At panShare, your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

**Important Notice**: panShare only provides an information indexing service for cloud drive sharing links. We do not store, host, or transmit any actual file content. All shared resources are stored on third-party cloud storage providers' servers (such as Baidu Pan, Aliyun Drive, Quark Drive, etc.).

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using panShare:

1. **Account Information**
   - **What We Collect**: This includes your username, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account.
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account, verify identity, and provide customer support.

2. **Shared Content Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Share titles, descriptions, cloud drive links, extraction codes, and other information you submit.
   - **Purpose**: To display and index shared content, making it easier for other users to find resources.

3. **Usage Details**
   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use panShare, including browsing history, search history, and access frequency.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user behavior and improve our services.

4. **Device Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access panShare, such as IP address, device type, operating system, and browser type.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize services, ensure security, and prevent abuse.

5. **Cookies**
   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences and login status.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance service functionality and personalize your experience.

## Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. The data we collect is stored securely on our servers, and we implement a variety of security measures including encryption and access controls to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except in the following circumstances:

- To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from public authorities.
- To cooperate with reasonable complaints and infringement investigations from copyright holders.
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.

## Third-Party Services

Share links on panShare point to third-party cloud storage providers. When you access these links, you will be subject to the privacy policies and terms of service of the respective cloud storage providers. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of relevant third parties.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "effective date" at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.
